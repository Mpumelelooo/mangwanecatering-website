ABOUT US

Mangwane Catering is all about bringing people together through delicious cooked meals.
We specialize in authentic African cuisine, crafted with the finest ingredients and love.
Whether it's a small gathering or a large event, our team delivers fresh, flavorful dishes
that make every occasion memorable. We take pride in offering personalized service,
ensuring every detail meets your needs.

At Mangwane Catering, we don't just serve food-we create experiences that feel like
home.

Let us make your next event unforgettable.







MANGWANE SERVICES







WEDDING
CATERING

We provide a tailored wedding catering
experience, including breakfast options such as tea, coffee, juice, scones, wraps
cakes, and sandwiches (optional).
Choose two starches (savory rice, dumplings, cream stamp, pap, or tieng),
two meats (beef stew, grilled chicken, fish, or more), three salads, and one
vegetable. A variety of desserts is also
included. Our Service covers groceries (optional), plates, cutlery, tables,
chafing dishes, serving, and cleaning.







PARTY
CATERING

For parties, we offer flexible menu options,
starting with breakfast selections like tea,
juice, wraps, and cakes. Choose two starches and two meats from
our range, three salads, and one vegetable.
Desserts and full service,
including plates cutlery, tables, and
cleanup, are provided, ensuring a seamless event.







FUNERAL
CATERING

We cater with sensitivity for funerals,offering two
starches and two meals, along with three salads and one vegetable from our menu.
No dessert included, respecting the occasion.
Our team handles groceries (optional), tableware, serving, and cleanup.







UNVEILING
CATERING

Our unveiling catering includes a variety of breakfast options
and a customizable menu with two starches, two meats, three salads,
and one vegetable. A dessert selection is also provided, along with
full catering service from
preparation to cleaning.
